## 1. Who we are

## 2. Our approach: we hold as little personal data as possible
Command360 is a **tool that organisations use to build and run their own training**. We are not a platform that profiles individual participants. Training participants generally **do not create accounts** and join sessions by link or QR code; we collect little or no identifying information about them. Where any participant data is handled, the **organisation is the data controller** and Command360 acts as its **processor** under a Data Processing Agreement.

## 3. The personal data we process (as controller)
- **Account & contact data** — for organisation administrators and users: name, work email, job title, organisation, and login data.
- **Billing data** — for paying customers (handled via our payment provider; we do not store full card details).
- **Usage & device data** — logs and analytics about how the service is used, at organisation/session level.
- **Outreach/prospect data** — for business contacts we approach: name, work email/role and organisation (see lawful basis below).

For personal data processed **on behalf of customer organisations** (e.g. session/participant data), see the relevant organisation's own privacy information and our Data Processing Agreement.

## 4. Why we process it and our lawful basis
- **To provide the service** (accounts, hosting, support) — *performance of a contract*.
- **To take payment** — *performance of a contract / legal obligation*.
- **To secure and improve the service** (analytics, fraud/security) — *legitimate interests*.
- **For business-to-business outreach** to relevant organisations — *legitimate interests* (we carry out and document a Legitimate Interests Assessment; you can object at any time).
- **To meet legal/regulatory obligations** — *legal obligation*.
