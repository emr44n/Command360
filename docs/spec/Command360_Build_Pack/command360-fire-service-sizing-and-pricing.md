# Command 360 — Market Sizing, Cost Modelling & Pricing Reference (v2)

> **Calibration anchor (hard data):** West Midlands Fire Service — **~2,500 staff, 38 stations** — pays **£20,578.64/yr for 8 xVR licences** (£2,572.33 each, 8% annual uplift baked in), plus a further licence in another department → **~£23k/yr total org spend**. And that spend is felt as *a lot*. Every price below is modelled against that reality.
> Staffing/station figures are **informed estimates — verify against each service's latest annual report/HMICFRS profile before quoting.** Competitor figures from the actual FY26 xVR renewal quote are exact.

## 1. The pricing logic — undercut what they ACTUALLY spend
An organisation's xVR pain = **licences held × £2,572**, compounding at 8%/yr. Typical holdings scale roughly with service size:

| Service scale | Typical xVR licences | Typical xVR spend/yr | **Command 360 price** | Year-1 saving |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Small** (≤ ~800 staff) | 2–3 | £5,145–£7,717 | **£6,000** | £1.7k+ (and unlimited users vs 3 seats) |
| **Standard** (~800–1,500) | 4–5 | £10,289–£12,862 | **£8,000** | £2.3k–£4.9k |
| **Large** (~1,500–3,000) — *the WMFS band* | 6–9 | £15,434–£23,151 | **£14,000** | **£6.6k–£9.2k** |
| **Metro/National** (3,000+) | 10–15+ | £25,723–£38,585+ | **£18,000–£22,000** | £8k–£17k+ |
| Bespoke private (aviation/COMAH/ports) | value-based | £6k–£7k+ (Effective Command anchor) | **min £8,000** | full creation platform vs playback toolkit |

Every band: **unlimited users (fair use), storage per band (25/50/100/150 GB), all features.** The per-licence story: WMFS's 8 seats cost £2,572 each; on Command 360 the *whole brigade* costs £14k — **£5.83 per operational member of staff**.

- **Uplift clause:** "CPI or 5%, whichever is lower" (vs xVR's 8) · **VAT:** quote ex-VAT (s.33 bodies reclaim; commercial pays) · PO/renewal cycle mirrored (reminders T-90/60/45).
- All prices are **defaults in admin Licence Profiles** — final numbers are set/curated per contract in the back end, not hard-coded.

## 4. Other sectors — sizing & value anchors (ESTIMATES — verify per site)
- **Aviation / RFFS:** ~40+ UK commercial airports maintain RFFS. CAT 9–10 (Heathrow ~200+ fire officers, Gatwick, Manchester, Stansted): **£10–15k**. CAT 6–8 regionals (20–60 RFFS staff — Birmingham, Edinburgh, Bristol, Luton, Glasgow, Newcastle…): **£8k**. CAT 3–5 small fields: **£6k**. CAP 699 competency evidence is the hook.
- **COMAH:** ~950 UK establishments, ~370 upper-tier (mandatory exercise regimes). Upper-tier sites (refineries, chemical works, fuel terminals): **£8–12k/site**; corporate multi-site deals negotiated. Exercise-evidence export is the killer feature.
- **Ports:** ~120 commercial ports; majors (Felixstowe, Southampton, London Gateway, Liverpool, Immingham) have port fire/emergency teams: **£8k**; harbour authorities smaller: **£6k**.
- **Rail/Metro:** Network Rail (route-level), TfL/LU, HS1, Elizabeth line ops, heritage: **£8–14k** by scale.
- **Defence/other:** MoD fire (DFR ~2,000 personnel): **N-band bespoke**; nuclear site licensees (~35 sites), power stations: **£8–12k**.
**Cross-sector reality:** private-sector sites budget £6–7k+ for Effective Command-style playback toolkits — Command 360 at £8k is a full creation/run/evidence platform. Value-price, don't cost-price.

## 5. Bandwidth & storage economics (why generous quotas are safe)
Storage costs ~$0.021/GB/mo → 150 GB National ≈ £30/yr vs £20k+ contract. Bandwidth: 30 orgs on daily video use ≈ 450 GB/mo; Cloudflare (free egress) caches media in front, Supabase serves only cache misses (~10–20%) → inside the included 250 GB. Fully uncached worst case ≈ $18/mo. The 50 MB/clip cap + client-side compression keep this true.

- **Licence Profiles (file 30):** these bands ship as editable templates in super-admin (price, users, GB, features, uplift %); every contract is curated per org at account creation. Pricing changes never touch code.
