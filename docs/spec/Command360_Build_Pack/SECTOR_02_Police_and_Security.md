# Sector 02 — Police & Security

*A large blue-light buyer with the most formalised command doctrine of all — Gold/Silver/Bronze originated here. Public-sector procurement (slow), but the competency frameworks are published, national and exhaustively prescriptive, which makes the content mapping clean. XVR already sells here (NCA, South Wales, Dyfed-Powys, Devon & Cornwall); the incumbent command-exercise tool is often Hydra/Minerva, which is facility-bound — Command360's browser delivery is the contrast.*

## 1. Snapshot
- **Target universe:** 45 territorial forces (43 England & Wales + Police Scotland + PSNI), 3 special forces (British Transport Police, Civil Nuclear Constabulary, MoD Police), the National Crime Agency, plus College of Policing training centres and regional public-order/firearms units.
- **Procurement:** Public sector — slow, framework-driven (BlueLight Commercial, force procurement).
- **Buyer / contact:** Head of Operational Training · force Public Order Public Safety (POPS) lead (ACC rank) · Firearms Training lead · Force Resilience / Contingency Planning · EPRR/civil-contingencies lead.
- **Edge over incumbent:** forces must reaccredit commanders *annually* and run frequent table-top and command exercises; a browser tool that runs these without a Hydra suite, and produces the decision-logged evidence, is cheap, scalable and bookable any day.

## 2. The mandate (the rigid guidelines)
- **College of Policing Authorised Professional Practice (APP)** — the official source of professional practice; Operations APP defines command and control.
- **Gold/Silver/Bronze (Strategic/Tactical/Operational)** — the nationally recognised command structure used by police, other services and partners; decisions must be documented as an **audit trail** (loggists support the command team).
- **Public Order Public Safety (POPS)** — accredited commanders + advisers (POPSAs); the **National Police POPS Training Curriculum (NPPOPSTC)**; reaccreditation is annual (bronze commanders/POPSAs do Level 2 public order training annually; gold/silver observe Level 2 tactics annually).
- **Firearms command** — Strategic / Tactical / Operational Firearms Commanders (SFC/TFC/OFC) trained under the **National Police Firearms Training Curriculum (NPFTC)**; the distinction between *occupational* and *operational* competence is explicit.
- **Competency & Values Framework (CVF) 2024** — the behaviours used for assessment and development.
- **Mobilisation / mutual aid** — NPoCC, the Strategic Policing Requirement; cross-force resourcing for large operations.
- **Civil Contingencies Act 2004** (Category 1 responder) and **JESIP** for multi-agency incidents.

## 3. Competencies to develop (worked backwards from APP/CVF)
- **Command decision-making** at gold/silver/bronze, using the **National Decision Model (NDM)** and recording rationale.
- **Public order command & tactics** — planning and commanding spontaneous and pre-planned events; PSU deployment; proportionality (Human Rights Act).
- **Firearms command** — threat assessment, authorising deployment vs authorising tactics, command of armed operations.
- **Major incident / CBRN / counter-terrorism command** — SCG/TCG, MTA response, working to the Strategic Policing Requirement.
- **Multi-agency / JESIP** — co-location, METHANE, Joint Decision Model with fire/ambulance.
- **Event & crowd safety planning**, control-room/command-facility operation, mobilisation/mutual aid.
- **Accountability & decision logging** — the audit-trail discipline that public inquiries scrutinise.

## 4. What they train on (scenarios)
Public disorder / protest / crowd · football & event policing · armed response & firearms operations · CBRN · counter-terrorism / marauding attack · major incident & SCG/TCG command · control-room / command facility · missing-person & area search · scene preservation & evidence management · custody.

## 5. How Command360 helps (competency → capability — SEO copy seed)
- **GSB command decision-making** → build the event/disorder scene, commit PSUs and assets, and let the crowd/incident evolve with the commander's choices; run it at gold, silver and bronze.
- **Decision logging & accountability** → every decision and its rationale captured automatically (NDM/JDM-structured), then replayed in debrief — the audit trail inquiries demand.
- **Public order & firearms command** → reusable, branching command exercises that satisfy the annual reaccreditation cycle without a fixed simulation suite.
- **Multi-agency / JESIP** → pull fire and ambulance commanders into the same browser session for joint major-incident exercises.
- **Assessment** → objectives tagged to APP role profiles / CVF behaviours; assessor view; results in the evidence export.
